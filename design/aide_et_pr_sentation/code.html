Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries.

Is this service free?

Yes, accessing the catalog and initial resources is entirely free, funded by public health initiatives.

Are the listed professionals vetted?

Absolutely. Every organization and individual listed undergoes a strict verification process by our health authority board.

What if I need immediate help?

If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a severe crisis, please use the 'Emergency' tab in the navigation or call local emergency services directly.
